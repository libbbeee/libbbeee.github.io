Root

My favorite boardgame at the moment is called Root by Leder Games. It is an asymetrical game where everyone plays different woodland factions and fight to rule over the forest.

Visit Leder Game's Website

The different factions you can play in the base game

  1. Marquise de Cat
  2. Eyrie Dynasty
  3. Woodland Alliance
  4. Vagabond

Collect wood to build infastructure in the woodland, which lets you grow your army and craft more supplies.
